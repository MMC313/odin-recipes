Steak

Description

After many failed attempts at cooking steak, MartianMooCow finally learned how to cook steak using the reverse sear method

Ingredients

  • 16 ounce, 1 inch thick steak
  • Paper towels
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Tablespoon of butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic

Steps

  1. Pat down steak with paper towel
  2. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder to your discretion
  3. Preheat oven to 200 degrees F, place steak inside the oven for 45 minutes
  4. While this is happening crush 2 cloves of garlic and cut a teaspoon of butter for later
  5. 1 minute before the steak is ready from the oven, begin heating a pan on high with oil until it starts smoking
  6. Take steak and press down onto hot pan, sear for 1 minute before flipping and add in butter and crushed garlic
  7. Baste the steak for 1 minute, and then take it out and let it rest for 10 minutes
  8. Enjoy medium rare steak
